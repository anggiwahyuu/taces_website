About Us
Telkom Annual Competition of English Society (T-ACES) is an annual event organized by Student English Society, a student organization at Telkom University. Since {2017}, T-ACES has successfully held a national English competition that uses English as its primary language and targeted senior high school and university students. There will be 7 competitive fields, including newscasting, debate, storytelling, scrabble, speech, and moviemaking.

What are you waiting for?!? Come and challenge yourself.

T-ACES will give you extraordinary experiences!

Competition
Short Movie
Short Movie competition, where the participants make a short movie with theme based on committee instructions. This field of competition aims to sharpen the participants’ imagination, creativity, and edit by creating an outstanding short movie.
Newcast
Newscasting competition, where the participants are going to deliver the news prepared by the committee as if they were a professional news anchor. This field of competition is aiming to increase the participants’ abilities in delivering the news in English professionally.
Scrabble
Scrabble competition, where the participants constructing words with a score for every words participants’ construct. This field of competition is aiming to enhance participants English Vocabulary and to improve their mathematical skill agility in constructing a words with a score in every letter.
Story Telling
Storytelling competition, where the participants will be telling a story in English while acting as the characters in the story and using equipment suitable with the storyline. This field of competition aim is to enhance the creativity, public speaking, and improve participants story telling agility.
Debate
Debate competition, whereas two teams will argue in regards to the motion that has been chosen. This field of competition aims to sharpen and develop a critical thinking and analytical thoughts on certain issues or topics, relying on data while delivering those thoughts and arguments in logical manner.
Speech
Speech competition, where the participants will be giving a speech based on committee instructions, which uses English as its language of instruction. The aim of this competition is to improve the participants’ ability in the way of expressing ideas about certain topics.
